Return Policy

We provide a straightforward return policy to address product concerns effectively.

Eligibility

Returns are accepted within 7 days of purchase. Products must be:

  • Unopened
  • Unused
  • In their original packaging

Non-Returnable Items

Opened items, custom orders, and temperature-sensitive products (e.g., refrigerated items) cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

  • Contact customer support within the return window.
  • Ship approved returns securely.

Conditions

Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the product is:

  • Defective
  • Incorrect

How to Request a Return

  1. Email or call customer support within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide proof of purchase and describe the issue.
  3. Return the product as directed by our team.
