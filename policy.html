Policy - RM Medical Agencies

Policy Overview

The purpose of this policy is to outline the principles and guidelines that govern our operations at RM Medical Agencies. As a medical supply provider, we prioritize customer satisfaction, quality assurance, and adherence to healthcare regulations.

Our Commitment

  • Transparency: Clear communication on product quality, pricing, and services.
  • Safety Standards: Ensuring all products meet or exceed regulatory requirements.
  • Customer Support: Providing solutions for queries, issues, and feedback in a timely and efficient manner.

Scope

This policy applies to all products and services offered by RM Medical Agencies, ensuring fairness and reliability in every transaction. It establishes processes for returns, refunds, and replacements to safeguard customer trust.
